Italian Recipes

Mushroom Ravioli with Spinach

Ingredients (2-3 Servings)

  • 500g of Button Mushrooms
  • Sun-dried Tomatoes
  • 500g of Spinach
  • 6-7 cloves of chopped Garlic
  • 750g of Ravioli Pasta
  • 4-5 Tsp of Olive Oil
  • 1 Stick of Unsalted Butter
  • Dried Chilli Flakes
  • Salt to taste
  • Italian Seasoning to taste
  • Creamy Sun-dried Tomato Sauce
  • You can use Alfredo Sauce or Marinara Sauce as a substitute
  • Serve it with any type of Meat, Salad, or Roasted Vegetables

  • Salt & Pepper to taste
  • Italian Seasoning to taste
  • Creamy Pasta Sauce made of Heavy Cream & Parmesan Cheese

Chicken Gnocci Soup

  • 1 Big Sized Onion nicely chopped

  • 2 thinly sliced Carrots

  • 500g of boneless Chicken cut into small chunks

  • Salt & pepper to taste
