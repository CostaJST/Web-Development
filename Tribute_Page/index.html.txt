        <h3>~ Here's a time line of Mother Teresa's life ~</h3>
        <ul>
          <li>1910 - born in Ottoman Empire as Anjezë Gonxhe Bojaxhiu.</li>
          <li>1928 - at the age of 18, left the home to join Sisters of Loreto in Ireland.</li>
          <li>1929 - arrived in India.</li>
          <li>1931 - took her very first religous vows.</li>
          <li>1937 - took here solemn vows while serving in culcutta as a teacher in Loreto Convent School. </li>
          <li>1946 - heard the call of her inner conscience to serve the poor of India for Jesus. </li>
          <li>1950 - founded the Missionaries of Charity, choosing a white sari with two blue borders as the order's habit.</li>
          <li>1960 - opened several hospices, orphanages all over the India and have done lots of charity work internationaly as well.</li>
          <li>1997 - after suffering from heart failure several times, she resigned as head of Missionaries of Charity and died on 5th sepetember in same year.</li> 
        </ul>
